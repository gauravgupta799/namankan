Overview:

Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS), Bangalore is a premier public university set up on June 1st, 1996 by the Karnataka Government. 

The university is in Bangalore, the capital of Karnataka. RGUHS is a promotional and regulatory body for medical science education in Karnataka. 

RGUHS, Bangalore is one of India's largest medical universities and is consistently ranked among the top medical universities in India. 

Rajiv Gandhi University of Allied Health Sciences affiliated colleges have been ranked consistently among the top Medical, Dental, and Paramedical Colleges in India. 

RGUHS is a member of the Association of Commonwealth Universities, United Kingdom (UK). 

Rajiv Gandhi University, Bangalore was established under the Karnataka State Legislature Act of 1994. 

The act encompasses all the existing medical science institutions in Karnataka. RGUHS ensures the best teaching, training, and research facilities for students in the modern and Indian medicine system.

Rajiv Gandhi University Courses for UG:
Medical: 
Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS):

Duration: 5½ Years (Including one-year of mandatory internship)

Eligibility:

The RGUHS applying candidates must pass 2 years of Pre-University or 12th standard examination with English as one of the subjects along with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as mandatory subjects. 

The candidates must secure a pass mark in all the subjects and a minimum of 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology combined. 

Dental: 
Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS):

Duration: 5 Years (Including one-year mandatory internship)

Eligibility:

Candidates must pass 2 years of Pre-University or 12th standard examination with English and Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as mandatory subjects. 

They must also pass in all the subjects and secure a minimum of 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology combined. 

Ayurveda: 
Bachelor of Ayurveda, Medicine, and Surgery (BAMS):

Duration: 5½ Years 

Eligibility:

The RGUHS applying candidates must pass 2 years of Pre-University or 12th standard examination. They must have studied English as one of the subjects and Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as mandatory subjects in their Pre-university or 12th standard.

They must secure a pass mark in all the subjects and a minimum of 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology combined. 

Homoeopathy: 
Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (BHMS):

Duration: 5½ Years (including one-year mandatory internship)

Eligibility:

The applying aspirant must have passed 2 years of Pre-University or 12th standard examination conducted by a recognized educational board. 

He/ she must have studied English as one of the subjects alongside Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as mandatory subjects. The total aggregate marks obtained by the candidate must not be less than 50%. 

Pharmacy: 
Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm):

Duration: 4 Years 

Eligibility:

The RGUHS applying candidates must have passed 2 years of Pre-University or 12th standard examination. 

The applicants must have studied PCM/ PCB/ PCMB/ PCME/ PCMC with an aggregate of not less than 50% marks.

The candidates who have passed the Diploma in Pharmacy course with not less than 60% aggregate are also eligible for the program and shall be permitted for the 2nd Year B.Pharm course. 

Nursing: 
Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) Nursing:

Duration: 4 Years (Including 24 weeks mandatory internship)

Eligibility:

Aspirants must have passed the Pre-University or 12th Standard examination. They must have passed the mandatory subjects, i.e., English, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology individually, with 45% marks.

Post Basic Bachelor of Science (P.B.B.Sc) Nursing:

Duration: 4 Years (including 24 weeks of mandatory internship)

Eligibility:

Interested candidates must have passed the Pre-University or 12th Standard examination in Arts, Science, or Commerce field offered by a recognized educational board. 

Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT):

Duration: 4½ Years (including 6 months of mandatory internship)

Eligibility:

Aspirants must pass the Pre-University or 12th Standard Examination with English as one of the subjects. 

The candidates must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as mandatory subjects with total aggregate marks of 45% or more. 

Allied Health Science: 
Bachelor of Science (B.Sc):

Duration: 3½ Years (including one-year of mandatory internship)

Eligibility:

Candidates seeking admission to B.Sc. in Allied Health Science Courses must pass their Pre-university or 12th Standard examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as mandatory subjects. 

Candidates with a 3-year Diploma in the preferred subject can also apply for the program. They should have completed their diploma from a Government-recognized Institute.

Rajiv Gandhi University Admissions:

RGUHS, Bangalore admissions are done via an online application process. Rajiv Gandhi University admissions application forms are released online on its official website.

Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences Admission Process:
  • The selection process for the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences courses (UG) is done based on the performance in the qualifying entrance examination.
  • The candidates appearing for the Rajiv Gandhi University admission for PG courses need to appear for the university's entrance exam 
  • For Ph.D candidates, RGUHS interviews the eligible candidates. 

Note:

Once the merit list has been released, the candidates should complete the RGUHS document verification process and pay the required fees by complying with the Rajiv Gandhi University fee structure for the admission process.

Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore Scholarships: 

Scholarship Objective: 

The RGUHS scholarship's main objective is to provide financial assistance for the meritorious and poor applicants to help them meet their expenditures while pursuing education at the university.
Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences scholarships are based on the results of the 10+2 examination or equivalent and the required specific criteria for the Undergraduate program.

RGUHS Scholarship Eligibility Criteria: 

The candidates applying for the scholarship must fulfil the following criteria:

  1. The student applying for the RGUHS scholarship must have studied for at least 10 years or more in Karnataka.
  2. MBBS and BDS students must have scored more than 80%, whereas the students of other health science courses must have scored more than 60% in 10+2 or other equivalent examinations.
  3. The RGUHS, Bangalore applying candidate’s parents' annual income must not exceed more than Rs. 6 lakhs per annum.
